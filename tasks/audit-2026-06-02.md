### Verification methodology

Findings were validated by: (1) `grep -n '#\[cfg(test)\]' <file>` to establish the production/test boundary, then comparing each cited line; (2) reading the actual construct (`unwrap_or` / `unwrap_or_default` / `unwrap_or_else` cannot panic; documented `.expect()` on an infallible builder is idiomatic); (3) trusting the deterministic tools (clippy, cargo-audit, rust-code-analysis, llvm-cov, jscpd, gitleaks) over subagent prose. No crate source was modified during the audit, so all numbers reflect HEAD with the working-tree changes present at scan time.
